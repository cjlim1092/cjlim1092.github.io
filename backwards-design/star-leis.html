Making Star Leis

Learning Objectives: Execute your plan and assemble your star lei.

Watch the last 3:00 minutes of the video twice. As you watch the video the second time, fill in the blanks in the directions below:

  1. Cut the thread ______ in long. Leave extra.
  2. _____ the needle, DO NOT tie a knot
  3. Poke the needle through the _______ of the side of a pentagon, the other end should come out the ______ of a star.
  4. Tie a knot to secure the star on the thread.
  5. Thread your needle on the ______ end of the thread. Fold the edge 6 inches over, but do not tie it.
  6. Add the rest of your stars to the thread, arrange them to alternate colors or however you like. You can also use beads between each star for spacing.
  7. After you have added all your stars, tie a knot to secure the end of your lei.
  8. Tie the ends of the ______ together to finish your lei!

On the last page you planned:

I want my lei to be the color(s) __________________________. I am going to make a lei for a child/ adult (circle one) so I will make ______ stars. I am using _______ (number) colors. So I will need ______ (number) stars for each color.

Let's do some more planning:

The color order of my stars will be _____ then _____. I will use a ______ needle and a _________ as my thread. I will cut my thread _______ in long.

Now try to make your star lei! Remember to be careful, needles are sharp. Make sure to ask for help if you need it, and don't give up! You can do it if you try.
