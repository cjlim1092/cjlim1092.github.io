Making a Paper Star Lei

The following are the learning objectives for this project.

  1. Students will be able to describe the attributes of a lei
  2. Students will be able to fold a paper star
  3. Students will be able to assemble a paper star lei

Leis are used to spread the spirit of aloha. They are often given as gifts to commerate special occasions. The paper star lei is suitable for elementary school graduations.
