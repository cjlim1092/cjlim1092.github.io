Making Paper Stars

Learning Objectives: Plan your lei and make a paper star.

Watch the first 2:30 of the video twice. As you watch the video the second time, fill in the blanks in the directions below:

Materials

  • 6 pages of printer paper
  • A lei needle or sewing needle
  • Thread, fishing line, or toothfloss
  • Scissors or a paper cutter
  • A ruler
  • A pen

Diections

  1. Using the ruler, mark your paper into ____ inch strips.
  2. Using your scissors, cut your paper into 60 strips for an ____ lei or 40 strips for a children's lei.
  3. Make a loop on one side of the paper and pull the end of the paper through the loop to make a knot. Tighten this knot into a ________ shape.
  4. Fold the small end of paper into the pentagon.
  5. Wrap the pentagon using the _____ end and fold along the bottom edge.
  6. When you get to the end, fold the strip into the _______.
  7. Inflate the star by using your ___________ and ___________ to pinch each point of the star,

Now that you know the steps, plan out your lei and gather materials.

I want my lei to be the color(s) __________________________. I am going to make a lei for a child/ adult (circle one) so I will make ______ stars. I am using _______ (number) colors. So I will need ______ (number) stars for each color.

Now try to make your paper stars! If you are new to origami, this might take a few tries. Make sure to ask for help if you need it, and don't give up! You can do it if you try.
