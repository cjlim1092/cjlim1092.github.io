Making a Paper Star Lei

Making a paper star lei is fun! In this DIY project you will:

  1. Describe the features of a lei
  2. Create a paper star
  3. Design and assemble a paper star lei

Leis are used to spread the spirit of aloha. They are often given as gifts to commerate special occasions in Hawaiii and on the mainland. The paper star lei is suitable for elementary school graduations, because kids love the playful colors and 3D paper stars. The best part is, paper star leis won't die like flower leis do!
